About Our Company

Inspiral is an Electronic Manufacturing Service (EMS) supplier, specialized in embedded system design.

Some of our services are:

  • schematic design
  • printed circuit board(PCB) layout
  • software design (bare metal, RTOS, embedded Linux)
  • test and measurement setup
  • CE/EMC validation
  • production

We are strong believers in Agile design and therefore have a very customer-centric approach.
